Projects

Here are two of my recent projects showing my skills in Technology Life

How does hardware work?

As part of a team project in SITE 1101, we constructed and tested basic logic gates (NOT, AND, and OR) using transistors and electronic components in the Net Lab. We recorded and edited a video demonstrating the step-by-step construction process and explained how each logic gate works, following lab safety and teamwork guidelines.

Hour of Code

As part of the SITE 1101 Hour of Code (Hour of AI) team project, we organized and conducted a coding and algorithm-learning session for a local community. We taught basic programming concepts using Microsoft MakeCode, actively engaged participants throughout the session, and documented the event in an edited video shared online to promote computer science education.
